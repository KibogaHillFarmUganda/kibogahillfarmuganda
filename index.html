Quality. Resilient. Ugandan Farming.

We keep, buy and sell healthy and quality breeds of goats, sheep, cattle, and poultry. We also grow, buy and sell coffee, bananas, maize, horticultural products, orchards and agro-forestry — we are powered by farming passion and driven by advancement in science. We also utilise weather and climate information for high crop yields and livestock production. We also carry out on farm educational visits, hold camps and research in collaboration with interested institutions.
