About Us

Kiboga Hill Farm Uganda blends modern agri-business with local methods. Led by professional scientists and managed by highly experienced workmanship, we integrate weather and climate information for decision making so as to minimise risks and losses. Among the strategies is to improve productivity by breed and variety choices. We collaborate with different individuals and institutions to carry out research and demonstration trainings.
